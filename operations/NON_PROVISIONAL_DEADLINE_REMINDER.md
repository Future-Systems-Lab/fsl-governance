# Non-Provisional Patent Conversion Deadline

## HARD DEADLINE: May 11, 2027

U.S. Provisional Application No. 64/063,037 expires 12 months from filing.
If a non-provisional application is not filed by May 11, 2027, the provisional lapses and the priority date is lost.
