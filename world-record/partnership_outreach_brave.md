## Summary

Future Systems Lab (FSL) is the first fully decentralized sovereign mental wellness ecosystem — and it was built exclusively for Brave Wallet. We removed MetaMask entirely and chose Brave as our primary wallet because its privacy-first, user-ownership model aligns with our sovereignty mission.

We believe FSL represents a compelling Brave Wallet showcase: a live, production platform with 5 integrated systems, 31 wellness games, and 8 smart contracts — all accessed exclusively through Brave Wallet's native EIP-1193 provider.

---

## Why We Chose Brave

1. **Native browser integration** — Brave Wallet lives in the browser, not as an extension. Fewer attack surfaces, cleaner UX for participants who are new to Web3.

2. **No phishing warnings on legitimate dApps** — MetaMask increasingly flagged our platform with misleading security warnings. We received phishing report #234718 on eth-phishing-detect despite being a legitimate educational platform. Brave Wallet has no such issue.

3. **Sovereignty alignment** — Brave is built on a privacy-first, user-ownership model. FSL is built on a sovereignty-first, participant-ownership model. The philosophical alignment is exact.

4. **EIP-6963 support** — We implemented EIP-6963 multi-provider discovery specifically for Brave. Our `personal_sign` consent gate works natively with Brave's wallet popup.

5. **MetaMask retired** — We made the deliberate decision to remove MetaMask from our codebase entirely. Brave Wallet is the only EVM wallet we support. This is not typical — most dApps support every wallet. We chose Brave exclusively.
