Dear Akash Network Team,

I'm building Future Systems Lab — the first fully decentralized sovereign mental wellness ecosystem. Five platforms, 31 therapeutic games, 8 smart contracts, wallet-only authentication, zero centralized payment processing. Live at encrypthealth.io.

This means Akash gets cited in an ASU doctoral thesis as the compute layer for the first decentralized wellness platform.
