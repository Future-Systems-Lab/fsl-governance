## Cross-Pollination Mechanics

### Track 1 → Track 2 Funnel
1. Wellness reader reads AOL article about sovereignty
2. Sees "Explore the technology at github.com/Future-Systems-Lab"
3. Clicks through to GitHub → sees smart contracts, open source PRs
4. Now understands FSL is both philosophy AND engineering
5. May share in developer communities, expanding reach

### Track 2 → Track 1 Funnel
1. Developer reads Dev.to article about decentralized wellness architecture
2. Sees "Experience sovereign wellness at encrypthealth.io"
3. Connects wallet → tries HypnoNeuro → experiences the platform
4. Now understands FSL is both engineering AND wellness
5. May share in wellness communities, expanding reach

### Why This Works
- Wellness publications won't cover "another blockchain platform"
- Tech publications won't cover "another wellness app"
- But "a naturopathic psychologist building her engineering thesis as a decentralized wellness ecosystem with 8 smart contracts" is a story both audiences want to read
- The dual-track approach ensures both audiences find their entry point
