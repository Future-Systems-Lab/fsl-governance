## Filing Day Steps (USPTO Patent Center)

### Step 1 — Access Patent Center
1. Go to https://patentcenter.uspto.gov
2. Log in with USPTO.gov account (create one if you don't have one — requires MyUSPTO registration)
3. Click **"File a new application"**
4. Select **"Provisional application"**

### Step 2 — Application Data
1. Enter title: "System and Method for Sovereign Behavioral Health Data Governance via EIP-191 Wallet-Signed Consent Attestation in a Zero-PHI Architecture"
2. Enter inventor: Margarita Davenport, NC, US
3. Select entity status: **Micro Entity**
4. Correspondence email: future.systems.lab@proton.me

### Step 3 — Upload Documents
Upload the following PDFs in order:
1. **Specification** (the main disclosure document)
2. **Claims** (if filing as separate document)
3. **Abstract**
4. **Drawings** (Figures 1-4)
5. **Cover Sheet SB/16** (filled and signed)
6. **Micro Entity Certification SB/15A** (signed)

### Step 4 — Pay Fee
1. Filing fee: **$65.00** (Micro Entity provisional)
2. Payment methods: credit card, deposit account, or EFT
3. Save the payment confirmation

### Step 5 — Review and Submit
1. Review all uploaded documents in the Patent Center preview
2. Verify inventor name, title, and entity status
3. Click **"Submit"**
4. **CRITICAL:** Save/screenshot the confirmation page

### Step 6 — Save Filing Receipt
1. Download the **Filing Receipt** (PDF)
2. Note the **Application Number** (format: 63/XXX,XXX for provisional)
3. Note the **Filing Date** (this is your priority date — 12-month protection window starts here)
4. Save to: `~/fsl-governance/academic/patent/FILING_RECEIPT/`
5. Backup to secure offline storage
