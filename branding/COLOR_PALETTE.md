## Primary Brand Colors

| Name | Hex | Role | Usage |
|------|-----|------|-------|
| **Aqua** | `#00D9FF` | Primary accent | On-chain elements, interactive highlights, links |
| **Violet** | `#7B2FBE` | Secondary accent | Phase 5 research, advanced features, AlchemistForge |
| **Gold** | `#FFD700` | Celebration / consent | Patent notices, consent highlights, EIP-191 auth |
| **Green** | `#22CC66` | Success / live | Deployed status, health checks, completion |
| **Amber** | `#FFB400` | Warning / pending | In-progress items, pending actions |
| **Red** | `#FF4444` | Error / exclusion | PHI exclusion markers, critical alerts |

## Paper Figures (Light Background)

For figures embedded in white-background academic papers:

| Element | Color | Hex | Weight |
|---------|-------|-----|--------|
| On-chain | Aqua | `#00D9FF` (fills at 15% opacity: `#E6F9FF`) | Borders at full, fills light |
| Off-chain | Slate | `#475569` (fills at 8% opacity: `#F1F5F9`) | Borders medium, fills very light |
| Consent/Auth | Gold | `#FFD700` (fills at 12% opacity: `#FFFBEB`) | Borders + backgrounds warm |
| Phase 5/Research | Violet | `#7B2FBE` (fills at 10% opacity: `#F3E8FF`) | Borders + labels |
| Exclusion/PHI | Red | `#FF4444` (fills at 10% opacity: `#FEF2F2`) | ✗ markers, exclusion panels |
| Success | Green | `#22CC66` (fills at 10% opacity: `#D1FAE5`) | Completion bars |
