# FSL Permission Bridge — Brand Mark Guide

## The Mark

### Circle — Wholeness (TCM Integration)

The circle represents wholeness in the Traditional Chinese Medicine sense — the complete, integrated system. Not "possibility" — completion. Health as integration, the taiji whole.

### Triangle — Three Load-Bearing Properties

The triangle represents the three load-bearing properties of FSL, all required, none optional:

1. **Autonomous** — no central authority
2. **Anonymous** — wallet addresses, no PII
3. **Secure** — cryptographic enforcement, not trust

Three sides, three properties. Pull one out and the structure collapses.

### Centroid + Cyan Ring — Bilateral Consent

The centroid and cyan ring represent bilateral consent. The meeting point where Sovereign Guide and Participant must both sign. Neither acts alone. This is the Phase 5 doctoral contribution rendered visually.

## For the Layperson

The circle is the complete system — whole and integrated. The triangle is what holds it up — three things that have to be true at once: autonomous, anonymous, secure. The point in the middle, with the ring around it, is where two people have to agree before anything happens. Neither one can act alone.
