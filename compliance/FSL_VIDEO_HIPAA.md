## Setup Steps (Each Practitioner)

1. Go to https://doxy.me
2. Click "Sign Up Free"
3. Choose username (recommend: dr[lastname])
4. In Settings → Enable HIPAA mode
5. Sign BAA (free, in Settings → HIPAA)
6. Share room URL with FSL for dashboard integration

## Session Flow

1. Participant books session via EncryptHealth
2. Participant receives practitioner's Doxy.me link
3. At session time: practitioner opens their Doxy.me room
4. Participant clicks "Join Session" → opens Doxy.me room in browser
5. Session conducts via encrypted video
6. After session: practitioner clicks "End Session" in FSL dashboard
7. Superbill auto-generates, SovereignLedger attestation written, HNT minted

## HIPAA Compliance Details

- **Encryption:** 128-bit AES encryption for video/audio
- **BAA:** Signed at no cost during account setup
- **Data retention:** Doxy.me does not store session video
- **Access:** Browser-based, no software download required
- **Audit:** Session timestamps logged by FSL SovereignLedger
